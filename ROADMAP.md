# Chornolis Marches Roadmap

This roadmap tracks planned gameplay systems and long-term direction for the project.

## Phase 1 — Atmospheric MVP

- Beginner onboarding and helper NPC.
- `/respawn` for lost early characters.
- Day/night cycle.
- Campfires and light sources.
- Hidden exits until inspection.
- Starter settlement and NPCs.
- Fishing.
- Compact news UI.

## Phase 2 — Survival

- Hunger and fatigue.
- Hunting and traps.
- Basic crafting.
- Barter and trade.

## Phase 3 — Living World

- Weather.
- Ecosystem expansion.
- Tracks and stealth.
- Slavic myth-inspired world events.

## Phase 4 — Society & Economy

- Reputation.
- Bounty system.
- Regional economy.
- Factions and caravans.

## Phase 5 — Deep Simulation

- Alchemy.
- Rituals.
- Construction.
- Endgame mysteries.
