## Prompt direction notes

Use these words when refining the style:

- Ukrainian / Slavic inspired, not generic Norse-only fantasy.
- Forest frontier, borderland, Порубіжжя.
- Ancient wood, mud, cold fog, lantern gold, red embroidery accents.
- Twisted branches, roots, carved border markers, watchtowers, palisades.
- Subtle spirits and silhouettes rather than explicit monsters everywhere.
- Semi-realistic, painterly, cinematic, symbolic.

Avoid:

- cartoonish style;
- glossy MMO splash art;
- modern corporate logo style;
- neon colors;
- clean high-fantasy castles;
- over-readable fake runes that look unrelated to Ukrainian ornament.
```
