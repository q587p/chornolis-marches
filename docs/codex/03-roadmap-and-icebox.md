# 03 — Roadmap and Icebox

This file consolidates ideas that were repeatedly mentioned or saved for later. It is not a strict release plan.

## Strong project pillars to keep visible

- Liminal frontier fantasy: the world exists on thresholds between settlement, forest, myth, and danger.
- Skill-based progression through use, observation, and apprenticeship instead of abstract character levels.
- Skills can be discovered by watching NPCs, animals, monsters, or mythical beings use them.
- Living ecology, not static mobs.
- Small social signals are core gameplay, not only flavor.
- Diegetic UI and incomplete information should matter before broad mechanics.
- Ukrainian language and folklore-inspired identity.

## Systems / features to consider

### Character and onboarding

- Better onboarding and Ukrainian name validation.
- Keep tutorial / dream onboarding as the NOW priority until the first core loop is taught.
- Random pre-generated names with uniqueness checks and approval of Ukrainian cases.
- Show name cases, approval, registration, in-game time, money where relevant.
- Sleep/dream tutorial after onboarding or as onboarding space.
- Option to skip tutorial by “waking up” if already familiar.

## Near-term priority after tutorial

Do not jump straight into full combat, deep crafting, economy or PvP after the tutorial. The next lane should strengthen the playable core loop:

- move;
- `Озирнутися`;
- `Роздивитися`;
- notice traces, sounds, beings and small changes;
- find, gather or take a small thing;
- spend stamina and rest;
- see the world act without direct player input;
- learn by watching NPCs, animals, monsters or spirits;
- use small social signals with nearby beings.

## Dream tutorial / guide ideas

Possible onboarding frame:

- The player begins in a dream or other-space.
- A helper spirit/animal/folklore figure guides them through movement, looking, examining, rest, time, gathering, etc.
- If the player already knows the game, they can wake up and skip.
- The dream map can be separate from the real world map.

Potential Ukrainian/Slavic helper archetypes discussed:

- домовик-like helper if focused on settlement/home;
- лісовик or forest-adjacent guide if focused on Chornolis itself;
- animal helper if a gentler tutorial voice is needed;
- liminal/dream-associated figure if the tutorial is explicitly oneiric.

## Bridge / supernatural motif

For a current map with a small bridge before settlement, consider subtle supernatural/folklore content around thresholds, crossings, water, and boundary-guarding rather than turning it immediately into a boss encounter. The bridge can reinforce the “Порубіжжя” theme: a mundane crossing that also marks entry into an older, stranger order.
