# Chornolis Marches — Game Design

## Core Pillars

### Atmosphere First

The world should feel ancient, dangerous and mysterious.

### Liminal Frontier / Порубіжжя

Chornolis Marches is not just a forest adventure. It is a borderland: between settlement and wilderness, day and night, human and spirit, safety and danger, known and unknown.

The word “Marches” / “Порубіжжя” should guide the tone of the game.

### Living Ecosystem

Creatures interact independently of players.

### Skills Grow Through Use and Observation

Characters improve by doing things, but also by observing beings better at those things.

Progression should feel like apprenticeship, imitation and lived experience rather than abstract level-ups.
