# 0.13 Closure and 0.14 Transition

## Purpose

This note defines when `0.13.x` should stop expanding and when `0.14.x` should begin.

`0.13.x` is the **Core Loop & Onboarding Stability** line. It should make the first real play session stable, understandable and atmospheric.

`0.14.x` is the **Night, Light and Firewood** line. It should make world time, darkness and light matter in ordinary play.

## Current Decision

Do not pull day/night into `0.13.x`.

The next `0.13.x` patch should be a closure and QA pass, not another major feature expansion. If the first-session smoke path works and no blocker appears, move to `0.14.0`.

After public `0.14.x` news is published, treat the `0.13.x` lane as closed for new feature work. It is still valid to fix regressions, deploy blockers or first-session breakage, but broad onboarding, hunter-loop, weapon, prepared-name and starter-prose follow-ups should stay in backlog or later milestones until the 0.14 world-time/light/visibility foundation is stable.

## 0.13 Closure Gate

`0.13.x` can close when this path works without confusion or hard blocker:

1. New player starts character onboarding.
2. Prepared and custom name flows are understandable.
3. Tutorial dream teaches movement, `Озирнутися`, `Роздивитися`, gathering/inventory, food, sitting/resting and waking without broad menu noise.
4. `/sleep tutorial` remains explicit and does not conflict with future ordinary `/sleep`.
5. The player can finish or leave the tutorial safely.
6. The first waking locations show enough context to continue exploring.
7. `/respawn` / `Повернення` works as an early safety valve, not fast travel.
8. Feature inspection has a clear two-layer flow: brief `look <feature>` and deeper `examine <feature>`.
9. AFK/end-session/proactive-message guards prevent Telegram spam while the player is away.
10. Scribe/admin tools needed for first-session support are documented and audited where dangerous.

## Recommended Last 0.13 Patch

Treat the next patch as something like:

- `0.13.26 — Core loop closure audit`

Scope:

- first-session manual smoke checklist;
- small copy/help fixes;
- planning status cleanup;
- no runtime day/night;
- no ordinary sleep implementation;
- no hidden presence;
- no weapon/NPC hunger expansion unless needed to fix a blocker.

## 0.14 Implementation Order

`0.14.x` should be split into small, reviewable slices:

1. `0.14.0` — internal world-clock planning and canonical scale: 13 lunar circles, 28 days per circle and elapsed real milliseconds as a rate, not real-world time of day.
2. `0.14.1` — stored world minute, daypart/moon helpers, heartbeat advancement and `/time` reading actual internal world state.
3. `0.14.2` — weather MVP: persistent weather type/intensity, weather duration and `/weather`/`/time` display.
4. `0.14.3` — shared light snapshot helper combining sun/daypart, moon illumination, weather and local active light.
5. `0.14.4` — shared visibility service skeleton.
6. `0.14.5` — darkness reduces location details, nearby beings, tracks and ground objects.
7. `0.14.6` — active light sources reveal what darkness hides.
8. `0.14.7` — хмиз/firewood loop polish.
9. `0.14.8` — first biome-aware foraging table.
10. Later `0.14.x` or `0.15` boundary — lying posture and ordinary sleep, only after posture/rest/time/light rules are stable.

## Why This Order

World time should exist before darkness behavior.

Moon phase is not deep calendar flavor here; it is the first night-light input.

Weather is not full ecology here; it is a persistent atmospheric/light input.

Visibility should exist before observation learning.

Light-source rules should be shared before adding more firewood, foraging, sleep comfort or hidden-presence systems.

Ordinary sleep becomes more meaningful after day/night exists, because waking, comfort, fire and danger can later depend on actual internal world state.

## Keep Out of 0.14.0

Do not include in the first world-time PR:

- hidden follower spirits;
- observation-learning mechanics;
- lucid dreams;
- ordinary sleep recovery loops;
- weapon/equipment expansion;
- NPC hunger/cooking behavior;
- full stealth.

These are valuable, but they become cleaner after world-time, visibility and light foundations exist.
