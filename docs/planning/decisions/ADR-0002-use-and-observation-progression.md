# ADR-0002: Skills grow through use and observation

## Status

Accepted

## Context

The project should avoid universal levels and abstract XP as the primary progression model.

## Decision

Characters improve through relevant actions, and may discover or improve skills by observing more skilled beings.

## Consequences

- Skill progression should be grounded in lived experience.
- NPCs, animals and mythical beings can become sources of knowledge.
- Observation requires attention, visibility, relevant attributes and understandable context.
- Some skills may remain impossible or very difficult to learn without the right foundation.
