# APIARY-004 — Bear Honey Loop Scaffold

## Summary

Prepare a future bear behavior loop around honey without redesigning predator ecology or shipping full bear combat too early.

## Scope

- Add den and bear signs only when routing, rest and flee behavior are ready.
- Let a bear smell honey, raid an apiary, get stung, flee and recover.
- Leave tracks, damaged wax or local signs for players to observe.

## Out Of Scope

- Full bear combat.
- Bear reproduction.
- Starter-camp raids.
- Generic migration AI.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Bear behavior is bounded and cannot camp-kill new players.
- Apiary signs are inspectable and diegetic.
- The loop uses existing routing/action systems where practical.
