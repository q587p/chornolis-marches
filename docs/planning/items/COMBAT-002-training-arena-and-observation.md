# COMBAT-002 — Training Arena And Combat Observation

## Goal

After the full combat system exists, add a dedicated arena or training ground where characters can practice fighting and learn by watching others fight.

## Direction

- Add an authored arena, training yard, or sparring ground once combat has real rounds, damage, defense, wounds, stamina cost and surrender/stop rules.
- Let players fight there in controlled conditions without turning ordinary travel into constant combat.
- Let nearby observers gain small canonical learning progress from watching meaningful combat, similar in spirit to gathering, freshening and route-observation learning.
- Keep observation progress bounded and cooldown-safe so repeated staged bouts cannot become a raw skill farm.
- Surface training through diegetic text: practice, stance, breath, scars on the yard, and people learning by eye, not raw level numbers.
- Consider irregular tournaments only after ordinary sparring works: seasonal matches, monthly/yearly titles, named winners, local reputation, and visible records on a board.

## First Pass Scope

- A safe training location or arena feature.
- Voluntary sparring or practice bouts only.
- Clear entry/exit/cancel rules.
- Small observation progress for watchers.
- No permanent prestige ladder until the base loop is stable.

## Out Of Scope Until Combat Is Ready

- Full combat design itself.
- PvP consequences, crime, guards or bounty systems.
- Automatic group combat.
- Weapon durability or repair loops.
- Economy rewards, betting, shops or prize payouts.
- Monthly/yearly champion titles before tournament cadence is tested.

## Open Questions

- Should early sparring use blunted/training weapons or ordinary equipment with damage softened by arena rules?
- Should the arena belong near the starter camp, a settlement, a future Sich-like group, or a separate frontier landmark?
- How should surrender, knockout, injury and healing work in a non-lethal training context?
- Can NPC/local trainers demonstrate useful forms without requiring complex combat AI?

## Acceptance Notes

- The arena remains blocked on full combat foundations and should not shape 0.15 combat-adjacent MVP work.
- Watching a fight can teach only after observation learning has bounded anti-grind rules.
- Tournament/title ideas are preserved as later polish, not first-slice requirements.
