# DREAM-004: Advanced group and place-spirit dreams

## Goal

Store the larger lucid-dream vision without pulling it into the near-term implementation.

## Possible Scope

- Multiple characters enter the same dream instance.
- Dream locations exist on a reserved low `z` layer such as `-13`.
- Players can learn ritual conditions, quest nuance or local history through dream action.
- Dream actions can appease, disturb or oppose place spirits.
- Special conditions can allow a rare item or mark to return from the dream.
- One participant waking early can change the remaining dream.

## Acceptance Direction

- Group dreams build on `Гурт`/follow foundations.
- Place-spirit effects build on hidden presence, omen and ritual foundations.
- Dream conflict does not require the full combat system at first; appeasement, fleeing, bargaining or attention tests may come first.
- The writing avoids Christian “soul leaves body” framing.

## Implementation Order

Keep in Icebox until group travel, hidden presence and dream instance foundations are stable.
