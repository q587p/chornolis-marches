# FISH-002: Riverbank Fisher Tiny Slice

## Goal

Seed the future fishing loop with one named fisher NPC or one visible fisher action near existing riverbank locations.

## First Scope

- Add a named fisher NPC on a small riverbank route OR add planning/docs first if code scope is too large.
- Visible actions only: checking water, preparing line, waiting, pulling line, cleaning small fish.
- Let players examine/observe the action for a hint if observe support exists.
- Keep tone quiet and atmospheric.

## Non-Goals

- No full fishing economy.
- No tool crafting.
- No barter.
- No full food integration.
- No full fishing system yet.

## Acceptance

- A player can encounter or witness a fisher-like action near riverbank/bridge locations.
- Text suggests fishing as knowledge of place and rhythm, not just a water button.
- No full fishing system is required.
- Tests/build pass.

## Example Text

```text
Рибар не дивиться на поплавок. Він дивиться вище за течією,
туди, де темне коло з'являється раніше, ніж вода торкається очерету.

Ви помічаєте: тут ловлять не там, де вода вже збурена,
а там, де вона тільки збирається збуритися.
```
