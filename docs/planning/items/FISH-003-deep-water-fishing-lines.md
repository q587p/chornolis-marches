# FISH-003: Deep-Water Fishing Line Seeds

## Goal

Prepare a small bank of fishing/deep-water lines for future fishing work so the water motif can support depth, silence and hidden knowledge without becoming constant exposition.

## Scope

- Record fishing/deep-water lines in the content bible.
- When fishing exists, use these only for deep water, dawn/dusk/night, unusual catches, repeated silence or authored riverbank spots.
- Keep practical fishing messages practical most of the time.

## Candidate copy

```text
Мілка вода дає дрібну рибу. Глибока — те, що не завжди варто витягати.
```

```text
Старий рибалка каже: якщо довго сидіти тихо, річка починає думати за тебе.
```

```text
Не все, що клює, є рибою.
```

## Out of scope

- Implementing fishing.
- Fish ecology.
- New items.
- Quest hooks.

## Acceptance

- The lines are documented and ready for future fishing implementation.
- No current gameplay behavior changes are required.
