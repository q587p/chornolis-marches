# NPC-008: Starter supply guards and watched beginner stores

## Goal

Make starter-camp and watchtower supplies feel watched and maintained instead of unattended free piles.

## Motivation

Player feedback after the watchtower/cache slices: a mouse reached the watchtower supplies, and the fiction immediately asked for guards.

The immediate system fix is animal movement boundaries, but a future world slice can make the camp more believable by assigning a guard, watchperson or quartermaster-like NPC to important beginner supplies.

## Future Shape

- Add a starter-camp/watchtower guard or supply watcher.
- Let the NPC comment when animals, predators or reckless characters disturb beginner-critical supplies.
- Keep the role diegetic: not a shopkeeper, not a tutorial popup, but someone who helps the camp remain usable.
- If shared supplies become limited stock, let the guard enforce soft etiquette before hard punishment.
- Consider later links to theft, hoarding, contribution records and newcomer-helper systems.

## Out Of Scope For Now

- Full settlement law.
- Combat patrol AI.
- Player punishment, jail, bounties or formal theft mechanics.
- Hard economy around starter supplies.
