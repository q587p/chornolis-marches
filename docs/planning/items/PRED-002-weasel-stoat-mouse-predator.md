# PRED-002: Weasel or Stoat Mouse Predator

## Goal

Explore a later small ground predator such as a weasel or stoat as another mouse-pressure tool.

## Why This Stays Later

A weasel/stoat is a good mouse predator, but it immediately wants more rules than the hawk slice:

- burrows, roots, tunnels or small passages;
- stealth and hidden movement;
- small-animal den behavior;
- possibly different visibility and tracking clues.

Adding it before those foundations risks turning a simple predator into another ad hoc creature.

## Future Direction

- Decide Ukrainian naming and lexicon forms before implementation: `ласка`, `горностай`, or a setting-specific variant.
- Consider mouse preference around `90`, with low or no rabbit pressure unless the ecology needs it.
- Tie it to burrows/root/tunnel systems once those exist.
- Add local signs and track-reading hooks instead of proactive spam.

## Out of Scope For Now

- Implementing the species as a simple copy of fox/owl/hawk without burrow or stealth thought.
- Adding new path types only for this predator.
- Player-facing pet/taming behavior.
