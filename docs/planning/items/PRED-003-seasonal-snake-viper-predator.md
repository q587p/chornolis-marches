# PRED-003: Seasonal Snake or Viper Predator

## Goal

Keep snake/viper mouse pressure as a later ecology idea, not an immediate answer to the current mouse problem.

## Why This Stays Cold

Snakes can eat mice, but they pull in several systems that should not be rushed:

- seasonal warmth and cold;
- daytime basking / night inactivity;
- poison and fear;
- swamp, sunny stone or grass-edge habitats;
- player danger readability.

## Future Direction

- Decide whether the first species is a snake, viper or setting-specific creature.
- Make activity depend on season/weather/daypart once those systems are mature enough.
- Use signs, tracks and careful examination before direct danger.
- Consider poison only after combat/condition rules can carry it safely.

## Out of Scope For Now

- Adding poison as a one-off.
- Making snakes a common hidden player trap.
- Using snakes as the first daytime mouse-pressure fix; `hawk` should come first.
