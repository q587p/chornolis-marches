# QUOTE-001: Liminal Signs and Quotation Layer

## Goal

Add a small canonical content/design layer for recurring signs, quotation guardrails and motif reuse so future owl, dream, fire, map and fishing copy stays coherent.

## Scope

- Add `docs/content/signs-dreams-and-quotations.md`.
- Link it from `README.md` and `docs/game_design.md`.
- Record the rule: direct external quotation is rare and threshold-bound; most echoes must be altered, diegetic and local.
- Record the placement decision for `«Сови не ті, ким здаються»`: dream/waking threshold, not ordinary starter-camp prose.
- Record player-facing Ukrainian seed lines for:
  - owl direct threshold quote;
  - owl camp/world echoes;
  - first uncertain observation feedback;
  - magical campfire examine layer;
  - fishing/deep-water lines;
  - NPC rumors.

## Out of scope

- New mechanics.
- Full observation-learning implementation.
- New owl ecology behavior.
- New dream map content beyond documentation.
- Any lore explanation of what owls “really are”.

## Acceptance

- The docs clearly answer where the direct owl quote belongs.
- The docs distinguish direct quote, in-world echo and conditional examine copy.
- The docs include at least one Codex-ready follow-up task for dream, owl, fire and fishing usage.
- README/game design/roadmap point to the new content bible.
- No player-facing line mentions Lynch, Twin Peaks, Heraclitus, Dante or Shakespeare by name.
