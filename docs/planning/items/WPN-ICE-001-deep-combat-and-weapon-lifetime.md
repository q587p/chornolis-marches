# WPN-ICE-001 — Deep combat and weapon lifetime

Status: icebox

## Goal

Store the larger combat/weapon ideas without letting them shape the MVP.

## Keep cold during 0.13–0.15

- Full combat rounds.
- PvP with witnesses, law and bounty consequences.
- Armor, shields, blocking and parrying.
- Dual wielding, offhand items and strict hand occupancy.
- Weapon reach, initiative and positioning.
- Bleeding, wounds, fractures and infection.
- Weapon crafting trees.
- Weapon quality, maker marks and rare materials.
- Looting equipped weapons from corpses.
- Regional weapon laws and guard reactions.
- Automatic group combat.
- Combat dreams, ritual duels and spirit weapon rules.

## Why this stays icebox

The near-term project promise is not a full RPG ruleset. Weapons should first make the world more readable: a hunter has a spear, a знахар has a sickle, a player can hold a knife, and corpse freshening requires a blade.

Deep combat belongs later, after visibility, light, hunting, traps, item instances, learning and social consequence systems are stronger.

## Review trigger

Review this when at least two of these are true:

- item instances exist;
- hunting/traps are a stable loop;
- visibility/stealth is stable;
- settlement law/reputation exists;
- hostile NPC combat is intentionally promoted;
- PvP consequences are being designed.
