# 0.10.10 — Predator kills in ecology stats

## Summary

This patch makes fox and wolf hunting more deliberate and makes predator-caused deaths visible in ecology statistics.

## Ecology

- Foxes and wolves now choose prey by species preference, age and HP vulnerability instead of taking the first herbivore in the location.
- Foxes prefer mice and vulnerable rabbits; wolves prefer rabbits and especially weak, young, old or wounded prey.
- Predator hunger recovery now uses prey food value: mice feed little, rabbit children/old rabbits feed partially, and healthy young/adult rabbits feed more.
- Fox and wolf lifecycle values are slower than the small herbivore loop, preparing the ground for later predator reproduction without runaway packs.
- Predator kills still leave corpses, reduce predator hunger and add temporary local danger that can scatter nearby herbivores.

## Stats

- `/stat` now shows predator-caused deaths for the recent ecology window and all-time predator kills.
- Telegram `/stat` includes predator deaths alongside old-age deaths.
- `/stat.json` exposes the new `predatorKills` counter.
- Individual creatures now track attack attempts, successful attacks and kills.
- Web `/stat` and Telegram `/stat` show the most successful hunters.
