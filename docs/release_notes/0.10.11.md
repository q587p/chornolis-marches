# 0.10.11 — Starter predators and predator ecology

## Summary

This patch makes foxes and wolves appear after seed/reset and adds a cautious first predator reproduction loop.

## Ecology

- Added starter predators to seed and `/reset`: a fox pair near `forest_07_02`, lone foxes near the dry luka and a remote wolf pair in `DEEP_FOREST`.
- Renamed the small herbivore ecology processor away from the rabbit-only name.
- Added very conservative predator reproduction after small-herbivore ecology and before ordinary carnivore ticks.
- Predator reproduction uses prey-unit thresholds: foxes count mice and rabbits, while wolves mostly count rabbits.
- `/tickGet` now documents fox/wolf reproduction, pregnancy-state limitations and prey units.

## Stats

- `/stat` and `/stat.json` expose fox/wolf births and prey-unit counters from world tick reports.

## Notes

- Persistent pregnancy is not stored yet. The MVP still creates offspring immediately on a rare successful reproduction check.
