# 0.15.2 - Owl pressure signs and tuning

Date: 12026-06-01

## Summary

- Added a fourth starter owl at the riverbank edge to cover the riverbank mouse-pressure pocket without placing a predator directly inside the breeding cell.
- Added four inspectable owl-sign landmarks with daypart-aware text: active night pressure, dawn retreat traces and quiet daytime hiding.
- Added predator performance grouped by species to scribe/admin ecology stats for post-deploy owl tuning.
- Updated owl/ecology planning and documentation for the `OWL-002` testing slice.

## Risks / Rollback

- Existing production worlds need a seed/map refresh to receive the new owl-sign landmarks and the extra starter owl.
- Four owls may be too much or still too little depending on live mouse pressure; use predator-by-species stats and mouse counts after deploy.
- Owl signs are visible local landmarks, so they make owl pressure more legible even when the owl is hidden during the day. Roll back the feature entries and starter owl row if the signs make the night ecology feel too explicit.
