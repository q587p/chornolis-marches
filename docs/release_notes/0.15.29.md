# 0.15.29 - Follow intent and starter guidance fixes

Date: 12026-06-04

## Summary

This release combines the first narrow follow-intent foundation with two small starter-safety fixes.

Follow intent lets a player mark one visible local player, creature or local character as the being whose movement they are trying to keep in mind. It is deliberately not auto-follow, not party/group movement, not a companion system and not a learning grant by itself.

The starter watchtower torch stand now has a narrow authored reflected-light exception, and the hidden cellar water-word passage can be triggered by manual speech even when Spirit Call has automatic actions queued.

## Player-Facing Changes

- Added `/follow <target>` for visible local targets.
- Added `/unfollow` to clear the current follow intent.
- Added Ukrainian and MUD-style aliases such as `слідувати за знахарем`, `йти за Нестором`, `триматися за Лукана`, `стежити за мишею`, `follow herbalist` and `відстати`.
- `/me` now shows a compact `Чужий слід: <name>.` line when a follow intent is active.
- The starter watchtower torch stand can be inspected and used at night through reflected glow from the unfading campfire below.
- Manual no-target cellar water-word speech now takes precedence over queued/running Spirit Call actions without disabling Spirit Call.

## Technical Notes

- Added `PlayerFollowIntent` with one active intent per player.
- Added `src/services/following.ts` helpers for future observation and track-learning slices.
- Follow intent stores a last-known target label and last-seen location, but ordinary players only see the label.
- Target selection reuses visible local text targets, so hidden and absent targets cannot be followed.
- The starter watchtower light fix stays data-driven through existing feature-provided light behavior.
- Manual cellar water-word handling cancels only interruptible Spirit Call/legacy `auto:` actions; ordinary manual queues stay on the normal queue path.

## Out Of Scope

- No automatic movement.
- No explicit "try to keep following" movement action yet.
- No group/party acceptance.
- No companion ownership.
- No combat assist.
- No track-learning progress yet.
- No new herbalist route behavior.
- No visible exit, button or return route for the cellar water-word passage.
- No auto-repeat of hidden passages such as the cellar water-word route. Future follow movement must require learned route knowledge or an independent trigger.

## Risks / Watchpoints

- Follow intent is intentionally only attention context. Future follow movement must handle darkness, stamina, queues, hidden passages and learned route knowledge separately.
- The torch stand now counts as a feature-provided light source for the watchtower location. This is intentional starter infrastructure; avoid copying the same flag to ordinary torch stands unless they truly provide ambient light.
- The cellar remains dark by design. If beginner guidance later sends players into the cellar at night, keep those hints separate from the watchtower-only light fix.
- Manual cellar water-word speech bypasses only Spirit Call actions. If a player has ordinary manual actions queued, the phrase remains ordinary speech until the normal queue processes it.
