# 0.15.47 - Travel group foundation - 12026-06-06

## Summary

- Added the first consensual travel group foundation.
- Let players create a road group, invite visible local players, accept or decline invites, leave, and disband.
- Added a helper to follow the group leader through existing follow intent.
- Kept follow assist separate and opt-in; group follow-leader setup does not silently enable it.

## Boundaries

- No automatic group movement.
- No shared combat, loot, inventory, learning or group chat.
- No hidden water-word route sharing or route replay.
- No target consent model for combat, mounts, NPC companions or map/content expansion.
- Leader leaving disbands the group for this MVP instead of promoting another member.
