# 0.15.50 - Continuous follow assist catch-up

Date: 12026-06-06

## Summary

- Added guarded post-arrival follow-assist catch-up after completed player movement.
- Let assist-enabled followers continue through fresh clear ordinary route-memory one step at a time.
- Marked catch-up actions with `follow-assist:catch-up` for debugging and guardrail checks.
- Added `slow:followAssist.catchUp` timing around the catch-up helper.

## Boundaries

- No group movement, shared combat, shared loot, shared inventory, party chat, hidden-route sharing, public skill sheet or new map content were added.
- Catch-up uses ordinary queued `MOVE` actions and never directly updates player location.
- Hidden water-word movement, dark or non-directional memory, stale memory, wrong-target memory, manual busy state and unavailable exits do not trigger catch-up.
- Travel group membership alone still does not move anyone; members must choose their own follow intent and opt in to follow assist.
