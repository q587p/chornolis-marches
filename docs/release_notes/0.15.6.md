## Risks / Rollback

- Creature `LOOK` now keeps a queued reason when one is provided. This is intended for richer non-player posture text, but any future caller that passes a noisy reason will make that noise visible.
- Starter shared-cache bulk contribution uses the normal action queue and cache capacity checks, so very large carried stacks may be queued in chunks rather than all at once.
- Passive hunger is intentionally slow and silent, but it changes survival pacing for players who spend long stretches on light actions or away from heavy work; tune the four-hour interval after live play.
- Owl camp safety is deliberately narrow: it protects `start_border_camp` and `start_border_watchtower`, not every nearby edge cell.
- Rollback: revert this patch to remove the cat watch helper, owl camp boundary, creature `LOOK` reason preservation, passive hunger marker and docs/planning updates; roll back the migration if the passive hunger field was deployed.
