# 0.16.11 — Fauna diversity pass

Date: 12026-06-08

## Summary

- Activated `hawk`, `frog` and `snake` as real seeded animal species with lexicon-backed Ukrainian forms.
- Added a small daytime hawk presence distinct from nocturnal owls: hawks are visible during day and hide/rest outside the daytime window.
- Rebalanced wet starter fauna so riverbank and willow floodplain starts lean on frogs and snakes instead of dryland rabbits, mice and foxes.
- Updated starter population-floor restoration so fallback restoration and Lisovyk restoration target planning can restore the new wet fauna groups.

## Technical Notes

- Added `STARTER_FROGS` and extended `STARTER_PREDATORS` with hawks and snakes.
- Added seed species definitions for `frog`, `hawk` and `snake` in `prisma/seed.ts`.
- Added `hawkDaytimeSyncPlan(...)` / `isHawkActiveDaypart(...)` alongside the existing owl nocturnal profile.
- Added narrow hawk/snake prey preferences without adding a new combat, reward, profession, economy or group movement system.
- `/reset world` now resets frogs separately and resets all species represented in `STARTER_PREDATORS`.

## Validation

- Added `scripts/test/fauna-diversity.cjs`.
- Updated starter-animal, population-restoration, Lisovyk-restoration and world-seed coverage for the new species and wet-zone groups.

## Risks / Watchpoints

- Frogs are seeded/restored wet prey only in this slice; they do not yet have a separate reproduction/spread loop.
- Strange-totem cap expansion remains `0.16.12`.
- Full Lisovyk character work and any possible `Поклик духа` (`/spirit`) connection remain `0.16.13` or later.
- No Prisma schema, migrations, WorldEventMarker, `/spirit`, mentorship, group movement or action-completion gameplay changes.
